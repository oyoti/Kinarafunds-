Available Trade Types

Six Ways to
Trade the Digits

All six digit trade types are available across all KNRA indices — choose your strategy and prove your consistency.

Over
Over
Predict the last digit of the tick will be strictly greater than your chosen barrier digit (0–9).
Under
Under
Predict the last digit of the tick will be strictly less than your chosen barrier digit (0–9).
Even
Even
Predict the last digit of the final tick will be an even number: 0, 2, 4, 6, or 8.
Odd
Odd
Predict the last digit of the final tick will be an odd number: 1, 3, 5, 7, or 9.
Matches
Matches
Predict the last digit of the final tick will exactly match the specific digit you select.
Differs
Differs
Predict the last digit will be different from your chosen digit. Broader probability, tighter payout ratio.

FAQ

Common Questions

What exactly is the consistency rule? +
No single trade type can account for more than 80% of your total profit. So if you trade only Over/Under and it makes up 85% of your gains, you fail the rule. You must show a diversified mix — Over, Under, Even, Odd, Matches, or Differs — throughout the challenge.
What does the 4% max risk per trade mean? +
Each individual trade or run cannot put more than 4% of your current account balance at risk. On a $500 account that means a maximum of $20 per trade. On a $1,000 account, a maximum of $40 per trade.
What are the KNRA indices? +
KNRA1 through KNRA7 are seven proprietary synthetic digit indices exclusive to KNRA Funded. They operate 24/7 with no dependency on forex market hours or economic events.
What profit target do I need to hit? +
You need to grow your account by 10%: $50 on $500, $100 on $1,000, $300 on $3,000, $600 on $6,000, or $1,000 on $10,000. This must be achieved across at least 10 trading days with a minimum of 5 profitable days.
Is there a time limit on the challenge? +
No upper time limit. You must complete a minimum of 10 trading days, but beyond that you can take as long as you need.
How are payouts processed? +
Withdrawals from your funded account are processed within 24 hours via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or card. Your challenge entry fee is also refunded with your first payout.
Can I use all six trade types on every index? +
Yes — Over, Under, Even, Odd, Matches, and Differs are all available on all seven KNRA indices (KNRA1–KNRA7) at all times, 24 hours a day.
